Burger

Description

Burgers are awesome. They are easy to make, they can be customized in many different ways, and they are delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup evaporated milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup minced onion

Steps

  1. Preheat grill for high heat.
  2. In a large bowl, mix the ground beef, bread crumbs, egg, evaporated milk, Worcestershire sauce, cayenne pepper, garlic, and onion. Form the mixture into 8 hamburger patties.
  3. Lightly oil the grill grate. Grill patties 5 minutes per side, or until well done.
