Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a popular Italian dish made with layers of cheese, meat, and lasagna noodles, all topped with a creamy tomato sauce. This recipe is easy to make and can be prepared ahead of time for a quick weeknight meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 (12 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 12 ounces cottage cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 16 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. In a skillet over medium heat, brown ground beef, onion and garlic; drain fat. Add tomato sauce, tomato paste, salt and oregano; mix well. Simmer uncovered for 10 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, cook lasagna noodles according to package directions; drain.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine cottage cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, and shredded mozzarella cheese.
  4. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  5. To assemble, in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish evenly spread 3/4 cup of sauce mixture. Cover with 3 lasagna noodles, 1 3/4 cup of cheese mixture, and 1/4 cup sauce. Repeat layers twice. Top with 3 noodles, remaining sauce, mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Add 1/2 cup water to the edges of lasagna; cover with aluminum foil.
  6. Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes. Uncover and bake an additional 10 minutes. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
